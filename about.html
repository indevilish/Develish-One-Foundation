About devəliSH One Foundation

Driving environmental conservation and social upliftment since 2025

Our Mission

To protect biodiversity, restore ecosystems, and promote sustainable practices that contribute to global environmental goals, while uplifting marginalized communities through education, healthcare, and skill development.

Our Vision

A world where humans live in harmony with nature, ecosystems thrive, and every individual has access to clean water, quality education, and dignified livelihood opportunities.

Core Values

Sustainability, transparency, community-led action, and accountability. We believe in measurable impact and complete financial openness.

Our Story

devəliSH One Foundation was incorporated on 7th April 2025 as a not-for-profit Section 8 company under the Companies Act, 2013 (CIN: U88900UP2025NPL220764). It was founded by a group of passionate environmentalists and social workers who witnessed firsthand the impact of climate change, plastic pollution, water scarcity, and lack of basic education in rural India. Starting from Shrawasti, Uttar Pradesh – one of the most backward districts – the foundation has rapidly expanded its reach through low‑budget, high‑impact initiatives such as seed ball plantations, rainwater harvesting, upcycling waste, and mobile health camps.

Our name “devəliSH” signifies devotion to a green, healthy, and equitable society. We work closely with local communities, schools, and panchayats to ensure our solutions are sustainable and culturally appropriate.

Our Impact Areas

We align our work with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to maximize contribution to national and global targets:

  • SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation – through rainwater harvesting and community filters.
  • SDG 12: Responsible Consumption – waste management and upcycling.
  • SDG 13: Climate Action – tree plantation and awareness campaigns.
  • SDG 15: Life on Land – biodiversity corridors and native species planting.
  • SDG 3 & 4: Good Health and Quality Education – health camps, hygiene kits, and learning centres.
