Our Environmental & Social Programs

Sustainable initiatives aligned with UN SDG 2030

SDG 15

🌿 Greening Earth Mission

How we contribute: We distribute seed balls, organize plantation drives, and partner with local communities to restore degraded lands. Our volunteers plant native trees, create urban forests, and monitor sapling survival rates.

Impact: Increases biodiversity, fights global warming, restores green cover.

SDG 2030: Life on Land.

85,000+ trees
42 ha restored
SDG 6

💧 Water Guardians Program

How we contribute: We build rainwater harvesting systems using old pipes & tanks, distribute clay water filters, and conduct community workshops on water conservation. We also rejuvenate ponds and stepwells.

Impact: Recharges groundwater, provides clean drinking water, makes villages water‑secure.

SDG 2030: Clean Water and Sanitation.

3M+ litres saved
200k+ beneficiaries
SDG 12

♻️ Waste Warriors Initiative

How we contribute: We upcycle waste (tyres, bottles into planters), distribute cloth bags, and run zero‑waste workshops in schools and communities. We also set up waste segregation hubs.

Impact: Reduces landfill burden, protects marine life, promotes zero‑waste lifestyle.

SDG 2030: Responsible Consumption.

2+ tons plastic
2 waste hubs
SDG 14/15

🦜 Biodiversity Conservation

How we contribute: We organize nature walks, build birdhouses from scrap wood, conduct species surveys, and work with forest departments to protect endangered species and their habitats.

Impact: Protects endangered species, maintains ecological balance.

SDG 2030: Life Below Water & Life on Land.

12+ species
Habitat restored
SDG 7

☀️ Renewable Energy

How we contribute: We conduct solar cooker workshops, build small biogas plants from old drums, and promote solar lighting in off‑grid villages through community funding models.

Impact: Reduces carbon emissions, cuts electricity bills.

SDG 2030: Affordable and Clean Energy.

Solar cookers
Biogas units
SDG 13

🎭 Nature Issues Awareness

How we contribute: Our team performs street plays (nukkad natak), creates social media reels in local languages, and conducts school workshops to spread climate awareness and inspire action.

Impact: Inspires community action, spreads climate reality.

SDG 2030: Climate Action.

50+ plays
Reach 1M+
SDG 3

🏥 Health for Everyone

How we contribute: We run mobile health camps, distribute hygiene kits, facilitate tele‑consultation, and partner with local clinics to provide free check‑ups in remote areas.

Impact: Reduces preventable diseases, reaches remote areas.

SDG 2030: Good Health and Well‑being.

50+ camps
10k+ patients
SDG 4

📚 Education for Everyone

How we contribute: We establish community learning centres using recycled materials, train volunteer teachers, provide scholarships, and distribute educational kits to underprivileged children.

Impact: Increases literacy, empowers girls, breaks poverty cycle.

SDG 2030: Quality Education.

5 hubs
500+ students
SDG 3/4

🏅 Sports for Fit India

How we contribute: We organize sports competitions (athletics, kabaddi, football, badminton) at village and district levels. We identify talented underprivileged players and provide them with coaching, kits, and financial support to compete at national and international levels, including the Olympics.

Impact: Promotes fitness, discovers hidden talent, empowers rural youth.

SDG 2030: Good Health & Quality Education.

12+ events
30+ athletes
