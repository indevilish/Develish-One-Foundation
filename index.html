About Our Foundation

Committed to environmental conservation through sustainable initiatives

Our Mission

To protect biodiversity, restore ecosystems, and promote sustainable practices that contribute to global environmental goals.

Our Vision

A world where humans live in harmony with nature, ecosystems thrive, and communities prosper through sustainable practices.

Our Approach

We implement projects that directly address climate action, life below water, life on land, and sustainable communities.

Our Story

Founded in 7th April 2025 by Mr. Vishal Kumar Pandey & Mr. Vimal Kumar Pandey CEO Sachin Singh under Section 8 company act 2013, dev…ôliSH One Foundation has been at the forefront of environmental conservation efforts across the globe. Our journey began with a small group of passionate environmentalists determined to make a difference.

Over the years, we've grown into an organization with projects in 15+ countries, supported by thousands of volunteers and partners who share our vision for a sustainable future.

WASTE WARRIORS INITIATIVE

Implementing zero-waste ecosystems across urban slums and coastal villages via recycling hubs, upskilling programs, and corporate plastic take-back partnerships.

GREENING EARTH MISSION
