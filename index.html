About Our Foundation

dev…ôliSH One Foundation is a registered environmental NGO dedicated to conservation efforts aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Our mission focuses on protecting biodiversity, restoring ecosystems, and promoting sustainable practices that contribute to global environmental goals.

We implement projects that directly address climate action, life below water, life on land, and sustainable communities.

Ecosystem Restoration

Reviving degraded habitats through reforestation and conservation efforts

Water Conservation

Protecting aquatic ecosystems and ensuring clean water access
